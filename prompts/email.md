# Email Marketing Prompts — Frametek

---

## Prompt 1: Welcome Email
```
Write a welcome email from Frametek to a new enquiry/lead.
Tone: Warm, professional, reassuring.
Include: Who we are, what we do, what happens next, and contact details.
Length: 150-200 words.
```

## Prompt 2: Follow-Up After Quote
```
Write a follow-up email from Frametek to a customer who received a quote but hasn't responded.
Tone: Friendly, not pushy. Offer to answer questions.
Keep it under 100 words.
```

## Prompt 3: Seasonal Promo Email
```
Write a promotional email for Frametek for [season/month].
Highlight a relevant service: [service].
Include a limited-time offer and clear CTA button text.
Tone: Upbeat and direct.
```

## Prompt 4: Review Request Email
```
Write a short email asking a happy customer of Frametek to leave a Google review.
Tone: Grateful and genuine.
Include a placeholder for the review link.
Keep it under 80 words.
```
